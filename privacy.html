Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Giftly Pro. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data according to global security standards.

1. Data We Collect

To provide our AI-driven suggestions, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your email address for secure login.

We use industry-standard encryption. Your account and preferences are managed via Supabase, ensuring top-tier infrastructure security. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Deletion

We respect your right to be forgotten. You can permanently delete all your data (recipients, wishlist, account) instantly from the app settings.
