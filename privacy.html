Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Giftly Pro. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience, including:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you register.
  • Recipient Data: Names, ages, and interests you save to generate gift suggestions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as search queries and clicks.
  • Device Information: Region, currency, and language settings to localize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide personalized gift recommendations.
  • Synchronize your data across devices.
  • Improve our AI suggestion engine.
  • Notify you of upcoming special occasions.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your data locally and secure communication (HTTPS) with our servers. Account data is managed via Supabase, ensuring high security standards.

4. Affiliate Disclosure

Giftly Pro participates in various affiliate marketing programs. When you click on a gift suggestion and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings menu in the app.
