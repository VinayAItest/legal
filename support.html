Frequently Asked Questions

How do the AI suggestions work?

Giftly Pro analyzes the recipient's age, gender, and interests to search our global product database. It then selects items that match both the occasion and your specified budget.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure Supabase authentication. You can permanently delete all your data at any time from the app settings.

Why don't I see specific stores in my region?

We prioritize high-trust local leaders (like Amazon India, HEMA, or Bol.com). If you are in a new region, we use global providers like Amazon Global to ensure availability.
